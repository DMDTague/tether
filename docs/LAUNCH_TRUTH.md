# Launch truth

The overhaul improves product coherence and removes several unsafe prototype defaults. It does not establish production readiness by documentation alone.

A truthful launch claim requires a committed deployable client, durable authentication state, migrations, provider credentials and compliance, monitoring, abuse operations, invitation-link testing, and real-device synchronization verification.

Until those exist, describe Tether as a serious shared-listening product prototype with a working FastAPI backend and interactive browser client.
