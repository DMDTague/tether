# Anti-slop rules

- Do not add a primary tab for an unproven secondary behavior.
- Do not show the same live-listening proposition in multiple competing rails.
- Do not ask for information before delivering value when it can be inferred or requested later.
- Do not animate every surface or vibrate every control.
- Do not turn people into follower, streak, or percentile dashboards.
- Do not display numerical match precision unsupported by real evidence.
- Do not place monetization between an invitation and another human.
- Do not call the repository production-ready without the committed client and operational proof.
