# Abuse and safety baseline

Shared listening can expose routines, availability, relationships, and location patterns. Safety controls must remain close to the person and session.

- Block and mute must take effect across presence, discovery, invitations, messages, and sessions.
- Reports must avoid analytics and replay capture.
- Ghost must immediately remove public availability.
- Knock must not disclose more context than the recipient has chosen to share.
- Broad distance bands must not be combinable into a precise location trail.
- Repeat unwanted invitations and Pulses require rate limits and recipient controls.
- Public beta requires a documented moderation and response process.
