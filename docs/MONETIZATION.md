# Tether monetization boundary

The moment of human connection is not the tollbooth.

The following remain free and immediate:

- Receiving and opening an invitation.
- Knocking on a listening session.
- Joining synchronized playback.
- Sending Pulse.
- Basic cross-provider recovery.
- Creating the automatic session memory.

Potential paid value belongs around additional control or preservation:

- Custom Stage appearances.
- Extended Memory Anchor history and exports.
- Advanced recaps and relationship summaries.
- Group-session administration and collaborative queue tools.
- Managed creator, event, or community rooms.
- Premium provider-matching diagnostics and recovery tools.

Any voluntary advertising or sponsorship implementation must use server-side receipt verification and remain outside the core listening path.
