# Notification policy

Notifications should surface meaningful opportunities for connection, not manufacture return visits.

Eligible notifications:

- A direct invitation or Knock response.
- A close friend opening their listening when the user opted into that relationship.
- A Time Capsule reaching its intended condition.
- A session failure or account/security event requiring action.
- A low-frequency relationship recap.

Do not notify for generic city trends, follower growth, ranking changes, or anonymous engagement bait. Users should be able to control notification categories and relationship-specific alerts.
