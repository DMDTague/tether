# Experiment policy

Experiments may reduce friction or improve reliability, but they cannot secretly weaken privacy or make joining contingent on monetization.

Every experiment requires:

- A customer-value hypothesis.
- A primary metric and guardrail metrics.
- An explicit event-schema version.
- A defined population and stop condition.
- Review for privacy, accessibility, and abuse risk.

Do not experiment with hidden location precision, contact-data exposure, nonconsensual replay, deceptive compatibility scores, or ads in the join path.
