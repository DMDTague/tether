# Product copy

## Positioning

**Be there in the song.**

Tether is the fastest, most intimate way to share the exact moment you are hearing.

## Core actions

- Open your listening
- Join now
- Knock first
- Leave a capsule
- Send a Pulse
- Continue listening

## Privacy

- Open Door — friends join instantly
- Knock First — you approve each join
- Ghost — listening stays private

The copy should use ordinary social language before proprietary vocabulary and explain technical recovery only when it becomes relevant.
