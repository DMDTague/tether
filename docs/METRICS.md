# Tether operating metrics

## North Star

**Meaningful Shared Listens per Weekly Active User**

A session qualifies when two or more real users remain synchronized for at least five minutes, perform at least one relational action, and do not immediately block, mute, or report one another.

## Core funnel

1. Live person or session shown.
2. Person or session opened.
3. Join, Knock, or Invite tapped.
4. Other user responds.
5. Playback synchronization succeeds.
6. Session survives thirty seconds.
7. Session survives five minutes.
8. A relational action occurs.
9. The same pair listens again within seven days.

## Service metrics

- Median app-open-to-shared-audio time.
- Join and Knock acceptance rates.
- Median join latency.
- p50 and p95 synchronization drift.
- Corrections per listening hour.
- Provider mismatch and session failure reasons.
- Sessions started from current playback.
- Repeat co-listener pairs.
- Seven-day recurrence after a first successful session.

## Guardrails

- Blocks, mutes, and reports.
- Ghost adoption and location-permission rejection.
- Notification opt-out.
- Exits within thirty seconds.
- Crashes and connection drops.
- Privacy changes immediately after exposure.
- Complaints about inaccurate discovery evidence.
