# Visual system

Tether uses semantic color rather than decoration without hierarchy:

- Violet: relationship and Tether identity.
- Teal: synchronization, availability, and successful presence.
- Amber: Knock, waiting, and asynchronous capsules.
- Rose/red: safety and destructive action.

One slow ambient layer may establish atmosphere. Live people and sessions may display presence motion. Consequential actions may respond. Celebration is reserved for joining, Pulse, and meaningful completion. Glass and blur are accents, not the default container treatment.
