# Tether product principles

## Product promise

Tether is the fastest, most intimate way to share the exact moment you are hearing.

The core loop is simple: notice someone listening, enter the moment, interact while listening, and preserve the relationship afterward.

## Ten-second Tether

The product target is shared audio in under ten seconds from app open. Current playback, saved privacy, likely listeners, and provider state should be ready before the user asks for them.

## Relationship over reach

Friends and repeat co-listeners outrank anonymous popularity. Profiles explain why two people may enjoy listening together instead of presenting follower-marketplace metrics.

## Evidence over magic

Discovery shows shared artists, listening rhythm, mutual context, and broad availability. It does not invent precise compatibility percentages.

## Quiet until meaningful

Ambient motion is slow and limited. Live presence, joining, Pulse, session completion, and errors receive stronger motion because those moments matter.

## Trust is product quality

Open Door, Knock First, and Ghost must describe actual backend behavior. Raw location is reduced before storage. Discovery never exposes contact details. Analytics cannot receive private content.

## Joining remains free

No ad, subscription wall, or education modal may interrupt an invitation, knock, join, Pulse, or synchronized playback. Monetization belongs around customization, memory depth, collaboration, exports, and managed rooms.
