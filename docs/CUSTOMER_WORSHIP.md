# Obsessive customer worship in Tether

Customer worship means eliminating effort around the moment users actually value. It does not mean maximizing feature count or collecting every possible datum.

For Tether, the organization should optimize for:

- Opening the app and immediately understanding what can happen.
- Entering shared audio with a person in under ten seconds.
- Recovering gracefully when providers or track versions differ.
- Remembering privacy, provider, and relationship defaults.
- Measuring failed joins and synchronization quality before adding new surfaces.
- Preserving meaningful relationships rather than manufacturing public engagement.

A proposed feature earns primary placement only when it helps someone notice a listener, enter the moment, interact during it, or preserve the relationship afterward.
